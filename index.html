Hello! My name is Raymond, and I am an Honors 2nd year studying Computer Science and Game Development at Northeastern University. As a passionate programmer, game designer, and musician, I love to pursue projects ranging all of these interests to the fullest extent.

I first started programming games and animations on Scratch when I was in middle school. Ever since, I've fallen in love with the art of programming. In high school, I created a CI/CD pipeline as part of my internship at Boeing, and I was also the software lead for my high school's FRC Team, where we placed semifinals in the L.A Regional 2022.

At NEU, I've continued to pursue all sorts of projects from game design to coding projects to music compositions, even some 3D modeling too (check out my projects below!) I've participated in ~10 game jams and 2 long term projects as a part of the Game Studio Club. I've also done frontend and backend development for NEU's Electric Racing Team, and have created various projects in C++ and Java.

I want to explore the intersection between coding and art, specifically the use of programming to generate ever-changing sonic and visual landscapes. As a long term goal, I want to combine my interests in game design, programming, and music together to create a unique form of expression.
