Who am I?

Hello! My name is Raymond, and I am an Honors 3rd year studying Computer Science and Game Development at Northeastern University. As a passionate programmer, game designer, and musician, I love to pursue projects ranging all of these interests to the fullest extent.

I first started programming games and animations on Scratch when I was in middle school. Ever since, I've fallen in love with the art of programming. In high school, I created a CI/CD pipeline as part of my internship at Boeing, and I was also the software lead for my high school's FRC Team, where we placed semifinals in the L.A Regional 2022.

At NEU, I've continued to pursue my curiosity to the fullest extent. I've participated in ~10 game jams and 2 long term projects as a part of the Game Studio Club. I've also done frontend and backend development for NEU's Electric Racing Team, and have created various projects in C++, Java, and JavaScript.

Outside of programming, I am also a prolific music producer and composer, who has contributed over 15 pieces spanning electronic, ambient, and classical to games and other media here at NEU.

I love to explore the intersection between coding and art, specifically the use of programming to generate ever-changing sonic and visual landscapes. As a long term goal, I want to combine my interests in game design, programming, and music together to create a unique form of expression.
